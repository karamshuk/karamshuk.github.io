About Me

I am a Senior Data Scientist at Skyscanner. My research is focused on analysis and modeling of large scale web and social media datasets. Previously worked on studying access patterns in BBC iPlayer (collaboration with BBC R&D), mining geo-tagged social media datasets (collaboration with the University of Cambridge) and modeling user preferences in Pinterest, Twitter and Foursquare. Co-founder and former CEO of Stanfy LLC.

Recent activities:

  • Mar 2017: Our paper on analysis of the About.me website is accepted at ICWSM'2017! [PDF]
  • Feb 2017: Our survey of peer-assisted CDNs is published in Computer Networks [PDF]
  • Jan 2017: A new paper on classifying public empathy with CrowdFlower and CharSCNN [PDF]
  • Nov 2016: Presented our new paper on measuring media bias at SocInfo'2016 [PDF]
  • Sep 2016: I have joined Skyscanner! Great milestone and exciting times ahead!
