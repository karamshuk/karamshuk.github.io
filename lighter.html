About Me

I'm currently working on a EPSRC project together with my advisor Nishanth Sastry where in collaboration with the BBC R&D team we look to improve content delivery infrastructure of the BBC catch-up TV system, BBC iPlayer, by analyzing access logs of users who collectively represent over 44% of UK's households [more here]. Previously, I've been working on analyzing human mobility patterns from location-based social networks [more here].

Projects I am/was involved in:

  • Empathy and Trust In Communicating Online (EMoTICON), ESRC project [learn more]
  • CD-GAIN: Content Delivery Through the Analysis of Users' Access Patterns, ESPRC project [learn more]
  • IX Summer School on Achievements and Applications of Contemporary Informatics, Mathematics and Physics, AACIMP'2014 [learn more]
  • The Sixth IEEE International Workshop on Network Science for Communication Networks, NetSciCom'2014 [learn more]

I got my master's degree in computer science at the National University of Ukraine "KPI" in 2007. From 2005 to 2010 I was working in the co-founded software company Stanfy LLC. From 2010 to 2013 I was a PhD student at IMT Institute for Advanced Studies, Lucca and worked under supervision of Dr. Marco Conti from the Institute for Informatics and Telematics of National Research Council (IIT-CNR), Pisa. In 2012 I did a research internship at the Computer Laboratory, University of Cambridge where I worked with Dr. Cecilia Mascolo.
